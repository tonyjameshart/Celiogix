Recipe Name: Gluten-Free Banana Bread
Category: Dessert
Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 60 min
Servings: 8
Difficulty: Easy

Description:
Moist and delicious gluten-free banana bread made with ripe bananas. Perfect for breakfast or as a snack. This recipe is naturally sweet and has a tender crumb.

Ingredients:
- 3 ripe bananas, mashed
- 2 cups gluten-free flour blend
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
2. In a large bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.
3. Add melted butter and mix well.
4. Stir in sugar, beaten eggs, and vanilla extract.
5. In a separate bowl, combine gluten-free flour, baking soda, and salt.
6. Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the banana mixture until just combined.
7. If using, fold in the chopped walnuts.
8. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
9. Bake for 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
10. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.

Notes:
- The riper the bananas, the sweeter and more flavorful the bread
- Can be frozen for up to 3 months
- Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days
- For a healthier option, substitute half the sugar with honey
